Sea Turtles

..are reptiles, and what makes them unique are their long, streamlined shells on their backs. Most scientists recognize seven species of sea turtles. Depending on the species, sea turtles color range can be olive-green, yellow, greenish-brown, reddish-brown, or black. Different species of sea turtles like to eat different kinds of food. They have mouths and jaws that are specially formed to help them eat the foods they like. Sea Turtles are a fundamental link in marine ecosystems and help maintain the health of coral reefs and sea grass beds.

Where?

You can find them in tropical and temperate waters throughout the world, like the Pacific, Indian, and Atlantic Oceans. They mostly swim in the ocean and rarely come on the land.

Fun fact

Sea turtles are one of the few living beings that survived the K-Pg meteor that made the dinosaurs extinct.
